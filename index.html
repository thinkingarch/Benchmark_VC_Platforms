The 2025 Market Landscape

Based on the Data Driven VC Landscape 2025 report, the ecosystem has bifurcated into four distinct operating models:

1. Old-School VCs

Rely on manual workflows and basic tools (Salesforce, Email). Risk: Dying breed (1% share).

2. Productivity VCs

Adopt off-the-shelf modern tools (Affinity, Notion, Zapier). Push for efficiency but lack proprietary edge.

3. Augmented VCs (Winner)

Build in-house solutions combining data with human intuition. Use Python, Airflow, and Specter. (94% adoption).

4. Quant VCs

Algorithmic models and autonomous agents. No human involvement in sourcing. Stack: Pinecone, PhantomBuster.
